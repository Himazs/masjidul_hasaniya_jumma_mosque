Serving Faith, Knowledge & Community

The Al-Masjidul Hasaniyya Jummah Mosque, located in Ethungahakotuwa, Udubaddawa, Kurunegala District, serves more than 1000 families. It hosts the five daily prayers, Jummah, religious sermons, Tharbiyya training, and a Qur'anic Madrasah for children.

Registered under the Wakf Department (No. R-626-KU-71), the mosque faces challenges such as space limitations and flooding during the rainy season. With approvals from the local council and the Department of Muslim Religious & Cultural Affairs, a new two-storey mosque (80×60 ft) for 2000 worshippers is under construction.

Serving Since 1971

Al-Masjidul Hasaniyya Jummah Mosque was established in 1971 to serve the Muslim community in Ethungahakotuwa village and surrounding areas. Over the decades, it has grown from a small prayer space to a vibrant community center serving over 1000 families.

The mosque is officially registered with the Wakf Department under registration number R-626-KU-71 and operates under the guidance of the Department of Muslim Religious & Cultural Affairs.
